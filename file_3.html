Our Story

Woven with Care,
Made to Last

Cloudweave was born from one simple belief: comfort should never compromise conscience.

Every blanket begins its journey with artisans in mountain villages, where generations of weaving knowledge meet ethically sourced fibers. We partner directly with 32 small-batch weavers across New England and the Pacific Northwest.

Our commitment to sustainable craft means slower production, fewer pieces — and a blanket you'll pass down for decades.
