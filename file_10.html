Our Story

Made with Love,
Worn with Joy

CozyNest was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a sanctuary. Our founder, Emma, started weaving blankets in her Vermont farmhouse kitchen — gifting warmth to friends and family through the long winter months.

Today, every CozyNest blanket is still made with that same intention. We partner with small mills in New England and Portugal, sourcing the finest merino wool, organic cotton, and recycled cashmere to create pieces that feel like a hug.

  • Certified organic & ethically sourced materials
  • Carbon-neutral shipping on all orders
  • 1% of revenue donated to housing charities
  • B Corp Certified since 2021
