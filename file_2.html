Our Story

Born from a Cold Night in Etchelroy

It started with a single blanket, made by hand in the small mountain village of Etchelroy. Our founder, exhausted by thin, scratchy store-bought throws, spent a winter perfecting the ideal weave — one that felt like a hug.

Today, we partner with family-run farms across New Zealand and Patagonia to source the finest merino fleece, woven in our small-batch studio by artisans who have been crafting textiles for generations.
