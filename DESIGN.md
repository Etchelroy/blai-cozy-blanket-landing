STYLE NOTES:

Primary Font: "Playfair Display" for all headlines — serif, elegant, warm editorial feel
Body Font: "Inter" for all body text, labels, buttons — clean, modern, highly legible
Font pairing creates contrast between luxury and approachability.

Letter-spacing: Headlines tight at -0.02em, uppercase labels wide at 0.15em
Line-height: 1.15 for large headings, 1.6 for body text
Section vertical padding: 96px top and bottom on all major sections
Inner container max-width: 1200px, centered with auto horizontal margin
Grid gap on product cards: 32px
Border-radius language: Consistently 16px for cards, 10px for inputs, pill (9999px) for CTA buttons in hero

Overall feel: Clean luxury with warmth. White space is generous. Blue tones shift from deep navy (authority, trust) to sky blue (lightness, comfort). No harsh edges. Every component breathes. The brand should feel like a cashmere sweater looks — effortless and premium.
