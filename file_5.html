Our Story

Born from a Cold Winter
and a Big Dream

CloudNest started in 2012 when founder Sarah Chen spent a particularly harsh winter in Vermont and couldn't find a blanket that was both beautiful and genuinely warm. She started hand-knitting for herself, her friends asked for one, then strangers on Etsy, and a brand was born.

Today we partner with small farms and artisan mills across New Zealand, Scotland, and the Peruvian highlands — always prioritising craft, ethics, and the kind of quality that gets passed down through families.
