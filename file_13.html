Our Story

Born from a Love
of Quiet Evenings

CozyCraft began in a small Edinburgh studio in 2016, when founder Sophie Reid couldn't find a blanket that was both truly soft and thoughtfully made. So she made her own — weaving together premium fibres, sustainable ethics, and a genuine passion for home comfort.

What started as a kitchen-table project has grown into a beloved brand trusted by over 24,000 homes across the world. But our values haven't changed: every blanket we make is still chosen with the same care, crafted with the same intention.

Why CozyCraft

Crafted to a Higher Standard

We obsess over every detail so you never have to — from the thread count to the packaging your blanket arrives in.

Temperature Smart

Our adaptive fibre blend regulates body temperature — warm in winter, breathable in summer. One blanket, every season.

Cloud-Soft Feel

Our micro-texture weave creates a surface so soft, customers consistently describe it as "the softest thing I've ever touched." We consider that high praise.

Sustainably Made

100% of our fibres are responsibly sourced, our packaging is plastic-free, and we offset every shipment through verified reforestation projects.

Machine Washable

Premium doesn't have to mean precious. All CozyCraft blankets are machine-washable and maintain their softness wash after wash, guaranteed.

Gift-Ready Packaging

Every order arrives in our signature recycled kraft box with a handwritten note and tissue paper — gift-ready from the moment it arrives.

Lifetime Guarantee

We stand behind every blanket with a no-quibble lifetime quality guarantee. If it ever doesn't meet your standards, neither does it meet ours.
